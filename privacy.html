The Vanadium Privacy Mandate

Our approach to privacy is uncompromising and architected from the ground up. We engineer AI solutions that operate exclusively within your designated secure perimeter. This means:

  • No Third-Party Clouds: Your AI models, proprietary data, and operational logs are never hosted on external servers. The phrase "the cloud is just someone else's computer" is a core tenet of our design philosophy.
  • Zero Data Sharing or Brokerage: We do not, under any circumstances, share, sell, or allow access to your data by any third party. Your information remains unequivocally yours.
  • On-Premise or Private Infrastructure Deployment: Systems are deployed on hardware you own and control, or within a private, isolated cloud environment that you exclusively manage.
  • End-to-End Encryption & Secure Protocols: All internal communications and data storage utilize robust, state-of-the-art encryption and security measures, audited and verified.
  • Minimized Attack Surface: By eliminating external dependencies and cloud vulnerabilities, we drastically reduce the potential vectors for unauthorized access or data breaches.
