Vanadium Agents (Licensable Professional AI Agents)

In addition to bespoke deployments, Vanadium continuously develops a suite of professional AI agents that excel at defined tasks. These are not generic tools. Each Vanadium Agent is ultra-professional, refined over time, and built to become the best in its class.

Organizations can license these agents, adapt them to their data and workflows, and deploy them on-premises for full control. The agents are continuously improved and offered with structured upgrade channels, but always under your governance.

Call Center Agent

Handles real-time customer calls, integrates with CRM, manages after-call work, and provides summaries and KPIs.

Legal Agent

Conducts rapid legal research, compares clauses, assists with drafting, and operates within configured risk thresholds.

Accounting Agent

Automates invoice processing, reconciliations, close checklists, and produces audit-ready trails.

Custom Industry Agents

Tailored for healthcare, logistics, procurement, manufacturing, and other specialized domains.

These agents are licensed with flexible models and deployed with policy-driven controls, ensuring every organization retains sovereignty while benefiting from state-of-the-art automation.
